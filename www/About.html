We're a small, diverse team that came together during covid to teach coding over Zoom to curious but stuck at home students. We offer apprenticeships to students who are typically left out of the opportunities in the tech world, especially at risk youth, women and girls, the BiPOC and LGBTQI+ communities.

As our student pool increased, so did out instructor pool and within a few short months, we we hosting hackathons as projects to teach students and to help small businesses and non-profits.

We teach general IT concerpts, networking, database development and management, cybersecrity, and web development. We teach using the case method where students are apprentices and attend to projects alongside the Project Managers. They are challenged to think through business problems to find the right solution for each unique businesses needs.
